    <section class="features">
      <h2>Key Features That Make CNC Wood Router Machines Essential</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>High Precision and Accuracy:</strong> CNC machines operate through computer-controlled commands, which ensures exact measurements and uniform cutting every time. In industries where even a millimeter difference matters, CNC routers provide unmatched consistency.</li>
        <li><strong>Faster Production Speed:</strong> Traditional woodworking methods require manual effort and take significant time. CNC routers automate cutting and carving, allowing businesses to produce large quantities at much faster speeds without compromising quality.</li>
        <li><strong>Ability to Handle Complex Designs:</strong> Delhi’s interior and furniture markets demand intricate carvings, 3D patterns, and decorative engravings. CNC routers can produce detailed artwork that is nearly impossible to achieve manually.</li>
        <li><strong>Versatile Material Compatibility:</strong> Apart from natural wood, CNC routers can work on MDF, plywood, acrylic, PVC foam board, particle board, and HDF — making them useful for signage, cabinetry, wall décor, and several industrial applications.</li>
        <li><strong>Reduced Material Wastage:</strong> Computerized cutting ensures the most efficient use of material, lowering waste and reducing overall production cost.</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Applications of CNC Wood Router Machines in Delhi</h2>
      <p>Delhi’s industrial landscape is diverse, and CNC routers play a vital role in several sectors:</p>

      <h3>1. Furniture Manufacturing</h3>
      <p>CNC routers are widely used to create sofa frames, table legs, headboards, wardrobes, modular kitchen components, and decorative panels. They provide smooth finishing and uniformity that enhance the overall quality of furniture.</p>

      <h3>2. Interior Decoration &amp; Architecture</h3>
      <p>Designer partitions, wall panels, jali work, ceiling patterns, and temple units are now commonly created using CNC routers. Their precision allows designers to explore creative geometric and floral designs.</p>

      <p>Business signboards, nameplates, and acrylic signages often require fine cutting and engraving. CNC machines help in producing clean edges and flawless details.</p>

      <h3>4. Wood Art &amp; Handicrafts</h3>
      <p>Delhi’s traditional handicraft market has adopted CNC routers to meet bulk order requirements and maintain artistic quality. Decorative items, customized gifts, and wooden artwork are now created with machine-guided detailing.</p>

      <h3>5. Industrial Prototyping</h3>
      <p>Many industries use CNC routers to create prototypes, molds, and machine parts. Because the machine can replicate the same design multiple times, it is ideal for commercial production.</p>

    <section class="types">
      <h2>Types of CNC Wood Router Machines Popular in Delhi</h2>

      <ul>
        <li><strong>Single-Head CNC Wood Router:</strong> Ideal for small businesses, this model is cost-effective and suitable for basic cutting and carving tasks.</li>
        <li><strong>Multi-Head CNC Router:</strong> Equipped with multiple spindles, this version allows users to produce multiple designs simultaneously. It is widely used for bulk orders and repetitive patterns.</li>
        <li><strong>ATC CNC Router (Automatic Tool Changer):</strong> ATC models automatically switch tools during operation, making them perfect for complex projects that require different cutting tools. They significantly reduce downtime and improve efficiency.</li>
        <li><strong>3D CNC Router:</strong> Used for deep carving, 3D models, statues, and detailed artwork. Interior designers and craftsmen prefer this machine for premium décor work.</li>
      </ul>
